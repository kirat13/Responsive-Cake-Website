What Makes our cake special

Canadian Blueberry Cake is a delightful dessert that encapsulates the fresh, vibrant flavors of blueberries, a popular berry in Canada, known for its wild and cultivated varieties.
The foundation of this cake is a simple, fluffy batter often made from staple ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, and butter. The magic happens with the incorporation of fresh or frozen blueberries that burst with flavor during baking, creating pockets of juicy sweetness throughout the cake. Often, lemon zest is added to the batter to complement the berries tartness, enhancing the overall taste profile of the cake.

What Makes our cake special

The "Hazelnut Gateaux Cake" is a luxurious and indulgent dessert, perfect for those who appreciate the rich, nutty flavors of hazelnuts. This exquisite cake features layers of moist, airy sponge cake, generously infused with a delicate hazelnut essence. Between each layer, a luscious hazelnut cream provides a smooth and creamy texture, complementing the sponge perfectly.

What Makes our cake special

The Duet Cake (White and Black Forest) from Just Bake is a spectacular fusion of flavours that harmoniously combines the richness of Black Forest with the delicate sweetness of White Forest. This exquisite cake features alternating layers of moist, chocolatey Black Forest sponge and light, creamy White Forest sponge. The Black Forest layers are generously filled with rich chocolate ganache, cherries, and a hint of kirsch, offering a delightful burst of flavour with every bite. The White Forest layers are equally tantalising, with smooth vanilla cream and fresh fruit that adds a refreshing contrast.
